LetSee: an App for Vision

Our aim is to develop an application to help visually impaired people. LetSee is an easy-to-use app to facilitate their lives, and help them to solve common recognition challenges. LetSee enables blind users to gain visual information by providing audio feedback. The current prototype recognizes banknotes, and it will be an even more useful toolbox for the blind in the future.

LetSee will provide endless possibilities to their users. Soon it will able to identify colors, recognize pictograph, read small displays on household appliances, recognize numbers of public transport vehicles, locate crosswalk, etc.

Explore the app

LetSee is exploiting the immense computational and sensory capabilities of smartphones.

Image Acquisition

LetSee uses the built in camera of your smartphone to look around in your surroundings processing each frame continuously.

Effective Processing

Fast and low-power algorithms running locally on your phone, thus no internet connection is needed to use the app.*

Audio Feedback

Once the app is highly confident in its decisions, results are immediately displayed on the screen and spoken out loud.

Task Selection

Since current version of LetSee recognizes banknotes only, accessible task selection menu will be introduced in a later release.

*But of course one can not download the app out of thin air ;)
